Abbreviation System

Variable abbreviations consist of 2-5 parts as listed below:

Type:


Property:


Nutrient:

Source:


Location:

Quantity, flow, increase, concentration, proportion, ...
Separated with space from next part

(in)digestible, (in)fermentable, soluble, absorbed, (un)degradable, ...
Three-letter abbreviations, precedes nutrient

Fibre, protein, starch, fat, ...

Originating from solid feed (forage, concentrate) or milk replacer (MR)
Only for relevant variables, separated with dash from previous part

Feed, rumen, abomasum, large intestine, ...
Separated with space from previous part

  • Property + resource are conjoined with a capital, e.g. degradable starch = degStch.
  • Abbreviations of 2 or more parts are conjoined with capital for second word (camelCase) or dash (depending on specific cases).
  • Type: Can be preceded by cum, avg or cavg for cumulatives, averages of cumulative averages respectively.
