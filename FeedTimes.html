How-to-use

Open the Feeding Times window through Menu bar → Settings → Feeding Times. Feeding times are only applicable for milk replacers.

Buttons

New: Define a new feeding time schedule.

Copy: Copy the selected feeding time schedule.

Delete: Delete the selected feeding time schedule.

Save: Save a newly defined or changed feeding time schedule.

Restore: Undo changes since last save.

Close: Close the Feeding Times window.

Fields

Name: Enter a name for the feeding time schedule.

Description: Enter a description for the feeding time schedule.

Times (0:10-23:50): Table to add different feeding times throughout the day.

How-to: Creating or adjusting feeding times

In the Feeding Times window, you can create schedules for daily feeding times of milk replacer in the simulation. There are four predefined schedules, namely:
  • Continous: Continous intake of milk replacer with a constant rate, representing a steady-state.
  • FourTimesADay: Milk replacer intake at four times throughout the day: 4:00, 10:00, 16:00 and 22:00 hr.
  • ThreeTimesADay: Milk replacer intake at three times throughout the day: 6:30, 12:00 and 20:30 hr.
  • TwoTimesADay: Milk replacer intake at two times throughout the day: 6:00 and 16:00 hr.
To create a new feeding time schedule, either click on the new button or select an existing feeding time schedule and copy it. Enter a name and description and fill in the feeding time, hours in the left column and minutes in the right column (24h notation). To add a new feeding time, place the cursor on the minutes of the last row and press ↓ (down arrow; only works if the time has been filled out for the last row). Once all feeding times have been filled out, click ✔ Save and the feeding time schedule can be used for the milk replacer schedules. Adjust an existing feeding time schedule by changing the hours and minutes that are already filled in. Delete a row with <Ctrl + Del>.
