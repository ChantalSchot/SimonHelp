AA limit, numbers amino acids

The variable named AA limit returns the number of the amino acid, limiting growth rate at a certain point in time.
If no value is returned, no essential amino acid is directly limiting. This does not imply that the growth rate achieved is optimal!

1 =threonine
2 = tryptophan
3 =valine
4 = methionine
5 =methionine + cystine
6 =isoleucine
7 =leucine
8 = lysine
9 =histidine
10 =phenylalanine
11 =phenylalanine + tyrosine
12 =arginine
