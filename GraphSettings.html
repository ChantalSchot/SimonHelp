Graph settings

After making one or more graphs, you can change the presentation of each graph by selecting the graph and click top-left on "graph" and "settings".
You can change the titles of the axis, change scaling and choose an appropriate spot for the legend.
The right part of this settings window shows all variables that are available for the graph.
If the variable of your choice is not available, you will have to make a new graph and make sure the table it is based on contains the variable you would like to see.
Sometimes, different output variables are difficult to combine in one graph.
In that case, make a new graph based on the same table.

You can also change the color and style of the lines used or the legend.
If you would like to change other settings, you can go for "more options", which invokes the graph generator used by the builders of this program.
Numerous options are available, which will not be discussed here.
